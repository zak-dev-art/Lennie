💞 A Love Benediction 💞

My Dearest Love,

As you walk into this new season, may heaven crown you with joy unending and peace that never fades. May your laughter echo with grace and your heart overflow with hope. 🌸

I thank God daily for the gift of you — your light, your strength, your beauty, and your faith. You are a reflection of Proverbs 31:25 — *“Clothed with strength and dignity, and you laugh without fear of the future.”*

I speak over you Numbers 6:24–26 — *“The Lord bless you and keep you; the Lord make His face shine upon you and be gracious to you; the Lord turn His face toward you and give you peace.”* ✨

And as for me, my heart makes this promise: I will walk beside you through every sunrise and storm. I will pray for you, believe with you, and love you in the pattern of 1 Corinthians 13 — *patiently, kindly, faithfully, and without end.* 💞

You are my answered prayer, and I vow to cherish you as God cherishes His beloved — with grace, honor, and truth. Song of Solomon 3:4 says, *“I have found the one whom my soul loves.”* That one is you. 💖

Forever yours, in faith, love, and prayer.
💋 — Yours, Always
